about
I'm currently pursuing a Masters Degree in Machine Learning at Carnegie Mellon University. I graduated from CMU's School of Computer Science in 2016. Over the past few summers, I have worked at Google, in Seattle and Mountain View, and Dropbox in San Francisco. I will be joining Google Brain in July 2017 as a full-time software engineer.

My research interests are deep learning, computer vision, reinforcement learning, generative models. I'm particularly interested in working with state-of-the-art convolutional architectures for object recognition and video tracking.

Outside of academia, I'm an amateur photographer and a fan of anything nature-related (hiking, camping, and anything new!).
