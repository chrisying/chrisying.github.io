about
I'm currently working as a Research Software Engineer at Google Brain. I graduated in 2017 with a M.S. in Machine Learning from Carnegie Mellon University's Machine Learning Department. Before that, I completed my B.S. in Computer Science (minor in ML) also at CMU. Over the past few summers, I've worked at Google in Seattle, Google in Mountain View, and Dropbox in San Francisco.

My research interests are deep learning, computer vision, and large scale machine learning. I'm currently working on research in efficiently utilizing extremely large amounts of compute to accelerate and improve current deep learning techniques.

Outside of work and academia, I'm an amateur photographer and a fan of anything nature-related (hiking, camping, and anything new!).
