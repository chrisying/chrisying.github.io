about
I'm currently pursuing a Masters Degree in Machine Learning at Carnegie Mellon University. I graduated from CMU's School of Computer Science in 2016. Over the past few summers, I have worked at Google, in Seattle and Mountain View, and Dropbox in San Francisco.

My research interests are deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. I'm particularly interested in designing large systems for performing machine learning at scale.

Outside of academia, I'm an amateur photographer and a fan of anything nature-related (hiking, camping, and anything new!).
